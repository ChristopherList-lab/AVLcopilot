Point your phone at the problem. Upload a photo of a fixture, a cable, a rack, or a cryptic error screen, and AVL Copilot guides you to the solution.

Knows Your Room

Load your rig into AVL Copilot and it learns your specific setup — every console, fixture, and signal path. Answers stop being generic and start being about your system.

Custom SOPs

Upload your own procedures and AVL CoPilot learns them. When a volunteer asks "how do I power on the sound system?", they get your answer — the one your tech lead already wrote. Ask once, answer forever.

Repair Routing

When something breaks, AVL Copilot tells you what you can fix yourself and when to call in help. Whether that's the manufacturer or a qualified local partner. No more Googling "[brand] service near me" at 9 p.m.
